ABOUT ME

I am a native Atlantan with a B.A. in Economics from Appalachian State University in Boone, North Carolina. I have spent my career working in the financial services industry specializing in corporate and public sector retirement plans.

After spending 20 years in a career that I fell into by chance, the timing was finally right to make a career change. I wanted to learn something completely new and something that could capitalize on my natural curiosity, creativeness, and problem solving abilities. I really enjoy learning about new things and I enjoy being able to apply what I've learned in the real world. Naturally learning about web development was an intuative step for me. So I decided to take the leap over to something completely new.

I currently reside in Atlanta with my wife and two boys.
